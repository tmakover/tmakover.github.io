        Setlist Changes and Concert Notes

        Christmas Song and Halloween added.

        Rye Whiskey cut, replaced with Sister.

        First time Spoon has been played since September 10, 2008. Its appearance makes 2010 the first year since 1998 to feature all songs from the album Before These Crowded Streets.

        Released as Live Trax Vol. 28 on December 17, 2013.

        #41 featured Joe Lawlor on electric guitar.

        John D'earth guested on trumpet on Warehouse and Jimi Thing.

        Jimi Thing featured Trombone Shorty on trombone.
