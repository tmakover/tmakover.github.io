The tour for 2005 saw the band promoting their new album, Stand Up. As a result, setlists at these concerts were naturally heavy with Stand Up material. Of special note for this year was the fact that I able to see the band play their staple of three-night shows at the Gorge Amphitheatre in George, Washington. The amphitheatre is situated near a cliff overlooking the Columbia River. It is a truly amazing venue! If you're interested in learning more about the venue you can visit their Facebook page by clicking here.

Another note of interest is that this was the first time I saw Dave Matthews Band perform on one of their Fall/Winter tours in Champaign, IL. I chose that stop in the tour since it was near to where father and stepmother lived in St. Louis.

  • June 1, 2005 - UMB Bank Pavilion, Maryland Heights, MO
  • June 28, 2005 - Verizon Wireless Amphitheatre, Charlotte, NC
  • August 19, 2005 - The Gorge Amphitheatre, George, WA
  • August 20, 2005 - The Gorge Amphitheatre, George, WA
  • August 21, 2005 - The Gorge Amphitheatre, George, WA
  • November 30, 2005 - Assembly Hall, Champaign, IL
