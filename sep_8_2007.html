        Setlist Changes and Concert Notes

        A Dream So Real and Dancing Nancies were cut and replaced with #40.

        American Baby Intro cut from start of encore.

        Happy Birthdyay was played in honor of saxaphonist's Leroi Moore's birthday.

        What Would You Say featured Warren Haynes on electric guitar.

        Melissa featured Gregg Allman on electric guitar and vocals.

        The event helped raise funds for the 53-acre expansion of Piedmont Park.

        Released as Live at Piedmont Park on December 11, 2007.
