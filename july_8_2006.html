        Setlist Changes and Concert Notes

        Where Are You Going cut after Dancing Nancies.

        The band played Neil Diamond's Sweet Caroline as its eighth song of the show as a homage to the tradition of playing it between the top and bottom of the 8th inning during ballgames at Fenway Park.

        Released as Live Trax Vol. 6 on September 28, 2006.
