        Setlist Changes and Concert Notes

        No setlist changes.

        Long Black Veil featured Mickey Raphael and Warren Haynes on harmonica and electric guitar, respectively.

        Cortez the Killer featured Andy Hess, Danny Louis, Matt Abts, Warren Haynes on bass, keyboards, drums, and electric guitar, respectively.

        All Along the Watchtower featured Andy Hess, Branford Marsalis, Danny Louis, Matt Abts, and Warren Haynes on bass, saxaphone, keyboards, drums, and electric guitar, respectively.
