2008 saw me returning to St. Louis, Missouri for the first time in a few years to attend the concert at the newly built Busch Stadium. The only other new venue for this year was the Virginia Beach Amphitheatre. That show was different from the others in that I didn't have in family in Virginia Beach and I bought tickets at the last moment, since I decided to attended just on a whim.

  • June 7, 2008 - Busch Stadium, St. Louis, MO
  • July 1, 2008 - Verizon Wireless Amphitheatre, Charlotte, NC
  • July 2, 2008 - Walnut Creek Amphitheatre, Raleigh, NC
  • July 11, 2008 - Cruzan Amphitheatre, West Palm Beach, FL
  • July 12, 2008 - Cruzan Amphitheatre, West Palm Beach, FL
  • August 7, 2008 - Verizon Wireless Virginia Beach Amphitheatre, Virginia Beach, VA
