        Setlist Changes and Concert Notes

        First time Minarets has been completely played since December 13, 2005, and the first time it has opened a show since June 12, 1994.

        First time Some Devil has not been played as a Dave Matthews solo since August 26, 2007.

        Branford Marsalis guested on saxaphone on Lover Lay Down, What Would You Say, and Jimi Thing.
