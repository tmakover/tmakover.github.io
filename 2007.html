2007 was a fairly typical but still special year of attending shows. It saw my second ever - and to this date last - Dave Matthews and Tim Reynolds show, as well as a special concert at Piedmont Park in Atlanta with close to 80,000 people in attendance. I think after last years plethora of shows, I unconciously reigned in the number of shows I saw that year, choosing to go to concerts where I only had immediate family nearby.

  • April 21, 2007 - William D. Mullins Memorial Center, Amherst, MA
  • September 8, 2007 - Piedmont Park, Atlanta, GA
  • September 14, 2007 - Sound Advice Amphitheatre, West Palm Beach, FL
  • September 15, 2007 - Sound Advice Amphitheatre, West Palm Beach, FL
  • September 18, 2007 - Alltel Pavilion at Walnut Creek, Raleigh, NC
  • September 19, 2007 - Verizon Wireless Amphitheatre, Charlotte, NC
