        Setlist Changes and Concert Notes

        No setlist changes.

        Typical Situation and Jimi Thing featured Branford Marsalis saxaphone.

        Candice Anderson, Sharon Bryant-Gallwey, and Tawatha Agee - known as the Lovely Ladies - guested on vocals for Be Yourself, Save Me, Rooftop, and Stay (Wasting Time).
